Lindt

A Swiss chocolatier and confectionery company founded in 1845 and known for its chocolate truffles and chocolate bars, among other sweets. At Lindt we pride ourselves on knowing that our cocoa beans are sustainably and ethically sourced. That’s why we implemented our own sustainable sourcing model: The Lindt & Sprüngli Farming Program.
